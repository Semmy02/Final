Rupiah Banda

Academic Background: Rupiah Banda studied at the University of Addis Ababa and later pursued diplomatic training.

Achievements:

  • Maintained economic stability during the global financial crisis.
  • Promoted infrastructure development, including road construction.
  • Strengthened Zambia's international relations.

Wife: Thandiwe Banda

Children:

  • James Banda - Born: 1965, Spouse: Unknown. Academic Background: Studied economics at the University of Zambia.
  • Henry Banda - Born: 1968, Spouse: Unknown. Academic Background: Attended school in Zambia and pursued further studies abroad.

Death: March 11, 2022, in Lusaka, Zambia. Cause: Colon cancer.
