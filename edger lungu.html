Edgar lungu

Early life and career Lungu was born 11 November 1956 at Ndola Central Hospital. After graduating with a LL.B. in 1981 from the University of Zambia, he joined the law firm Andrea Masiye and Company in Lusaka. He subsequently underwent military officer training at Miltez in Kabwe under Zambia National Service (ZNS). He then returned to practising law. He then joined politics. In 2010, Edgar Lungu had his law practicing licence suspended by the Law Association of Zambia. This was after he was found guilty of professional misconduct.

Achievements:

  • Implemented social protection programs like the Social Cash Transfer Scheme.
  • Promoted infrastructure development, including the construction of the Kenneth Kaunda International Airport terminal.
  • Strengthened Zambia's role in regional and international organizations.

Wife: Esther Lungu

Children:

  • Tasila Lungu - Born: 1985, Spouse: Unknown. Academic Background: Studied business administration at the University of Zambia.
  • Chiyeso Lungu - Born: 1987, Spouse: Unknown. Academic Background: Attended school in Zambia and pursued further studies in law.

Status: Alive (as of October 2023).
